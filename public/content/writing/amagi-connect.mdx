1 Mar 2022 - 18 Apr 2023

## Objective

Amagi, a global SaaS leader in broadcast and streaming TV technology, has launched "Amagi Connect," an online marketplace for platforms and content partners. The new platform will allow broadcasters to connect with content creators and license content through an intuitive, user-friendly interface. This marketplace will help streamline the content discovery and licensing process, ultimately making it easier for broadcasters to find and distribute content on their channels.

## Role and Responsibility

1. Design an MVP to experiment with designs that match user needs.

2. Create a design system for both light and dark UI.

3. Develop a dashboard with analytics views that allows users to compare which channels or platforms work better.

4. Build a finance module that tracks all invoices and payment due dates, with month-over-month analytics and support ticket generation.

5. Design a delivery tracker that allows customers to easily track their media deliveries.
